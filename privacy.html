Last updated: May 2026

01 Who We Are

SHAKEapp Inc., 13930 Expanding Light Dr, Nevada City, CA 95959, USA operates Holly.ai, an AI voice receptionist platform for medical clinics.

Contact: contact@shakeapp.today  |  hollyai.vercel.app

02 Data We Collect

During AI-assisted calls we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Phone number
  • Reason for medical visit
  • Preferred doctor
  • Appointment date and time

03 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Schedule appointments
  • Send confirmations via WhatsApp / SMS
  • Maintain appointment records
  • Improve AI accuracy (anonymized data only)

We never sell personal data to third parties.

04 Legal Basis

  • Colombia: Ley 1581 de 2012 — patient verbal authorization during call.
  • Panama: Ley 81 de 2019 — consent via requesting the service.
  • USA: Legitimate interest and contractual necessity.

05 Data Retention

  • Appointment data: 24 months
  • Call recordings: 90 days
  • Deleted within 30 days of a written deletion request

06 Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time, and to revoke consent with no penalty.

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@shakeapp.today

07 International Transfers

Data is stored in the United States (Supabase, SOC 2 certified) and protected by standard contractual clauses in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

08 Sub-processors

We use the following sub-processors, all located in the United States:

  • Supabase — database storage
  • Retell AI — voice processing
  • Google Calendar — appointment scheduling
  • Meta / WhatsApp — appointment confirmations
  • Vercel — application hosting

09 Contact & Complaints
